Virtual Flight

You must have the latest quicktime player properly installed on your computer to view the Virtual Flight.

You will start off inside the balloon getting a firsthand look at the inflation process, click and move your mouse to look around, When your cursor turns to a white arrow, you know that you can simply click to see another part of our virtual flight, look toward the basket and click to go outside of the balloon.

Point your mouse to the sky and click to see what it is like up high above beautiful Napa Valley in one of our balloons.

If you get tired of leisurely wafting amongst the clouds you can click on the landing and be back on the ground again.



QuickTime 5 Required
