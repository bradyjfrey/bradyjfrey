Bently Reserve

Client Launch: October 2006-2011
What I did
Art Direction
Lead Design
Front-End Development
Back-End Development

After 2007, I was given free reign to redesign Spider Ball into a more modern, organic, and stylized feel. The focus was to merge with the Burning Man arts community, and yet keep with a distinct feel.

I redesigned the logo to play well off the name, and created visual layers of depth to form flame between organic layout.
