Vermin, MC

Vermin MC - SF

Client Launch: May 2011
What I did
Art Direction
Front-End Development
Back-End Development
Lead Design

Website design & code for a new San Francisco Biker Club, Vermin, MC – SF. The design was free reign on my part, with the exception being the logo and the content. I designed textual treatment to the logo, though to give an image of a real patch, complete with photoshop cloth and sewing. Stitching, leather, smoke, and other novelties are all photoshop magic, typography is my custom work based off my appreciation for tattoo artwork.

Although only base html5, css3, and php, future expansions will include a calendar of events and group forums.
