Time Clock Icon

Bently Holdings

Client Launch: July 2011
What I did
Art Direction
Logo Design

Designed for my new, native, iPhone Time Clock Application – this icon set includes multiple variations for future desktop designs as well.

Due to small ring at the top for the traditional pocket watch, in various areas requiring a perfect square layout (such as Apple’s Dock and the iOS screen launcher), I’ve angled the icon to better fit to scale in a square size.

For the native application itself, a default vertical design fits just fine. The iOS application icon animates it’s time based on the internal clock of the iPhone.
